Jane Park

Jane Park was born in Antarctica and grew up sledding with penguins. She's since worked as a scientist, polar explorer, and barista. Now she lives in Nottingham with her two German Shepherds, Robin Hood and Maid Marian. Her hobbies including medieval reenactment and stealing from the rich. Her short fiction has been published in Darkspeed, Familiar Horizons, and Asimov Would Have Hated This. Protectors of the North is her first novel.
